Hello, I'm Ferdi - a freelance web application developer

About me

Hey, I'm Ferdi, a web developer from Italy with over five years experience in building web apps that run in the browser and on mobile devices. Currently, I'm building a tailor-made in-browser IDE for creating interactive online courses at DataCamp.
I have been working on this project over the course of two years from multiple places in the world.

Remote work gives me the freedom to work from the place I feel most productive in and made me and my team come up with some workflows that fuel focus, productivity and effective communication.

In the past, while living in Austria, I have built the online-shop for Billa, one of the biggest supermarket chains in the country. Other projects I have worked on include a web-based mobile app for a global pharmaceutical company called Novartis, as well as websites for smaller products and companies and many personal side projects that mostly didn't make it to production ;-)

Aside from coding, I'm a very active person. I enjoy activities like snowboarding, scuba diving, kite surfing, riding mountain bikes and everything that has to do with adrenaline and speed. I also enjoy taking photos and videos of my adventures and edit them. Some of it can be seen on my Instagram, @sifferdi.

If you would just like to say hi, or think you could use my help on something awesome you're working on, please get in touch.
