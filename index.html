About me

Hey, I'm Ferdi, a full stack developer from Italy with close to ten years of experience in building applications that run in the browser and on mobile devices. At the moment I am working on multiple web projects at Decidio.

Remote work gives me the freedom to work from the place I feel most productive in. Over the years, together with various distributed teams, I figured out strategies and workflows that fuel focus, productivity and effective communication.

In the past, while living in Austria, I have built the online shop for Billa, one of the biggest supermarket chains in the country. Later I joined DataCamp, where I built software that helps facilitate online learning in the data science field. Currently, I am building multiple different applications at Decidio, the one I'm most excited about being Truendo, a privacy management solution. Aside from that, I am working on a few side projects that unfortunately don't get too much attention at the moment ;-)

When I'm not sitting at the laptop, I'm a very active person. I enjoy activities like snowboarding, scuba diving, kite surfing, riding mountain bikes and everything that has to do with adrenaline and being outside. I also enjoy photography and videography.

If you would just like to say hi, or you think that you could benefit from my skill set, please get in touch.
